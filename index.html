Upgrade Your Check-In

Get stronger analytics, reporting, and retrospective attendance input for your Rock RMS check-in.

Analytics

Add custom badges to your user's profiles that track their attendance to specific groups and use custom report blocks to identify who's attended recently, attenders incorrectly marked inactive, and which groups users have attended.

Retrospective Attendance

Keep track of some groups on paper? No problem.
Rapidly add large volumes of paper based atttendance to Rock with our unique block designed with scale and speed in mind.

Track Headcounts

Want to keep a headcount as well as a person-by-person record of attendance? We did too. Record the heacount for each of your groups and compare them with the recorded attendance of your users.
